PROJECT DETAILS

Project Title

         Bike and Scooter Rental System

Overview of the Project

         People wants to travel from some distance with comfort. For 
  that we are rendering them our bikes, scooters and also our cars to 
  them to travel to places where they want to travel.
          Goals of the Project
                  To create a platform where seller and client can connect with 
             each other for business perspective.
                  And where vendors can post their bike or scooter and admin can 
             also manage all part in the system.
